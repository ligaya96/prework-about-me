!DOCTYPE html> My Favorite Bands Paramore Bio!

Paramore formed in 2004. The band currently consist of lead vocalist Hayley Willaims, guitarist Taylor York, and drummer Zac Farro. With the deptarture of Josh Farro as lead guitar(2010), and bassist Jeremy Davis (2015) The band second album Riot! was released in 2007, was a mainstream success and became a platinum hit. The bands 2009 follow up album, Brand New Eyes, was the second-hightest charting album to date. The single The Only Exception went platinum in several countries, as well gold in various countries including US. Following the departure of brothers Josh and Zac Farro, the band released their fourth album in 2013. A self-titled album, gave the band their first number one on the US billboard, and with the singles "Still into you" and "Aint it fun" won the band their first Grammy win.
